About HealthStrat

Founded by senior healthcare leaders, HealthStrat provides strategic advisory services to healthcare organisations across Asia.

HealthStrat is a Singapore-based healthcare strategy and training consultancy supporting healthcare organisations across the region in strengthening patient safety, workforce capability, clinical governance, and sustainable healthcare transformation.

We partner with hospitals, community care providers, healthcare leaders, and training institutions to design practical, future-ready solutions that improve care delivery and patient outcomes.

We believe healthcare transformation must go beyond strategy documents and translate into meaningful clinical and operational impact.

Our approach combines systems thinking, leadership insight, evidence-based practice, and practical execution to help organisations strengthen care delivery, workforce capabilities and long-term sustainability.
